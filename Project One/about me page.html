Let me
introduce myself...

I am a senior working towards my BFA of graphic design at
Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I am originally from
New York, but am currently living in Boston, MA. I love print and
UX/UI design, and always try to translate my love for stories
and making a difference into my work. I have interned at Boston
Magazine, and am currently interning at Small Army, both
located in Boston, MA.

On the rare occasions that I am not behind my computer
screen you might find me reading a good book, crocheting
a warm blanket, or binging new episodes of Ellen.
